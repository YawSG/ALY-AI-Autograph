About ALY AI: Autograph

Revolutionizing intellectual property protection with cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Yaw Sakyi-Gyinae, Adam Haycock, and Louis Dunleavy, ALY AI emerged from a shared vision to democratize intellectual property protection.

As innovators ourselves, we recognized the challenges that creators face when navigating the complex landscape of patents and copyrightsâ€”high costs, technical complexity, and time-consuming processes.

Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to create a fair, transparent, and accessible system that empowers creators of all sizes to protect their innovations with the same level of sophistication previously available only to large corporations.

Transforming IP Protection

ALY AI: Autograph is a legal-tech platform that leverages artificial intelligence to streamline the process of filing patents, copyrights, and intellectual property registrations. We serve everyone from independent inventors and startups to established corporations seeking efficient and cost-effective protection for their innovations.

Our Advanced Features

AI-Assisted Patent Drafting

Our AI automates the process of drafting patent applications, reducing time and cost for inventors while ensuring all technical requirements are met.

Prior-Art Detection

Our AI performs comprehensive searches for prior-art, ensuring that your invention is novel and reducing the risk of future legal disputes.

IP Portfolio Management

A sophisticated tool for managing and protecting a wide range of patents and copyrights, perfect for scaling organizations.

Patent Litigation Support

Our AI assists legal teams with managing and resolving patent disputes through data-driven insights and predictive analysis.

Originality Verification

AI-powered similarity detection allows creators to verify whether their work is truly original before submission, preventing potential conflicts.

Blockchain Proof of Ownership

Optional blockchain-based timestamping provides immutable proof of creation date and ownership for your intellectual property.

Our Ethical Commitments

Transparency

We ensure complete transparency with all users regarding their personal data and business with our company. Customers have the right to access and view any of their data that we hold, with the assurance that data can be deleted at their request.

Fairness

Our transparency policy underpins our commitment to prevent false results. We ensure every customer is treated fairly and equally. If you believe our copyright infringement prevention system has made an error, you always have the right to view the decision-making process and appeal the result.

Security

We are dedicated to protecting all of our customers' data with industry-leading security measures. Part of this security effort includes making IP protection accessible to small-scale investors and startups, democratizing access to legal protection.
