Privacy Policy

Please read our privacy policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal data.

By using ALY AI: Autograph, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing this policy whenever you access our services to stay informed about our data practices.

  1. Data We Collect

    ALY AI collects and processes the following types of information:

    • Personal Information (name, email address, contact details, and payment information) for account creation, service delivery, and billing purposes.

    • Invention & Intellectual Property Data related to your innovations, designs, and creative works submitted for patent and copyright filings.

    • Usage Data including log files, device information, browsing patterns, and other analytics that help us improve our platform and user experience.

    • Communication Data containing records of correspondence when you contact us or interact with our customer support team.

  2. How We Use Your Data

    We use your data for the following purposes:

    • To provide and improve our AI-powered intellectual property filing and management services.

    • To process transactions and ensure secure payment processing.

    • To personalize your experience and deliver content and product offerings relevant to your interests.

    • To communicate with you about service updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.

    • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and enhance the security of our platform.

    • To comply with legal obligations and intellectual property regulations.

  3. Legal Basis for Processing

    We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

    • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations to you when providing our services.

    • Legitimate Interests: Where processing is in our legitimate interests, such as improving our services and ensuring platform security.

    • Consent: Where you have explicitly given consent for specific processing activities, such as marketing communications.

    • Legal Compliance: Where we need to process your data to comply with legal obligations under UK and EU law.

  4. Data Protection & Security

    We implement robust security measures to safeguard your data, including advanced encryption for sensitive data, strict access controls, regular security assessments, and compliance with industry standards for data protection.

    While we are committed to protecting your information, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage users to take appropriate precautions when sharing sensitive information online.

  5. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information with:

    • Service Providers: Third parties that help us deliver our services, such as payment processors, cloud hosting providers, and analytics services.

    • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, including compliance with the Regulation of Investigatory Powers Act 2000.

    • Business Partners: In connection with specific services or joint offerings, always with appropriate data protection agreements in place.

    • Professional Advisors: Including lawyers, auditors, and insurers where necessary for the operation of our business.

  6. Your Rights

    Under the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and GDPR, you have several rights regarding your personal data:

    • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data.

    • Rectification: The right to request that we correct inaccurate personal data.

    • Erasure: The right to request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances.

    • Restriction: The right to request that we restrict processing of your personal data.

    • Data Portability: The right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

    • Objection: The right to object to our processing of your personal data.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in section 12.

  7. Data Retention

    We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When determining appropriate retention periods, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal data, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure, the purposes for which we process your data, and legal requirements.

  8. International Data Transfers

    ALY AI: Autograph primarily processes data within the United Kingdom and European Economic Area (EEA). However, some of our service providers may be located outside these regions. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses approved by the UK Information Commissioner's Office, adequacy decisions, and data processing agreements that enforce equivalent data protection standards.

  9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

    Our platform uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance functionality and user experience. These may collect information about your browsing activities and preferences. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, although this may affect certain features of our service.

  10. Children's Privacy

    Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

  11. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
